How to Use

  1. Use the search bar to find and select an item you want to produce.
  2. Enter the desired production rate (items per minute).
  3. Click "Calculate" to generate the production chain.
  4. Drag nodes to rearrange the graph.
  5. Click and drag on the background to pan the view.
  6. Scroll to zoom in and out.
  7. Click on a machine's recipe to switch between alternatives.
